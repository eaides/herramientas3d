# Summary

DuPont wires commonly used in Arduino\Raspberry Pis now have a group connector. 

This is a 24 pin wire block that can be plugged in or out, retaining the order of the wires.

Wires can be reclaimed and used for other projects simply by removing the cover and popping them out.

Updated the female connector to include more space for mounting screws.
